Skills & Talents of Data Scientists

Explore the key skills and talents you will learn with us. You will require them to succeed in the world of data science.

Core Skills

Statistics & Mathematics

Understanding probabilities, distributions, and linear algebra for data analysis.

Programming

Proficiency in languages like Python or R to manipulate and analyze data.

Data Wrangling

Cleaning and organizing raw data to prepare it for analysis.

Data Visualization

Creating charts, graphs, and dashboards to communicate insights effectively.

Domain Knowledge

Understanding the specific industry context to interpret data accurately.
