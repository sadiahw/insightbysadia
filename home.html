What is Data Science?

Data Science combines statistics, computing, and domain knowledge to extract valuable insights from data. It helps organizations make data-driven decisions for better outcomes.

Applications of Data Science

Finance

Risk modeling, fraud detection, and portfolio optimization using data analysis.

Healthcare

Early disease detection, personalized medicine, and hospital resource optimization.

Retail

Customer behavior prediction, recommendation systems, and supply chain optimization.
